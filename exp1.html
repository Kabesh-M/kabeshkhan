About the Project

The "Clean Neighborhood, Responsible Waste" project aims to tackle improper waste disposal by promoting responsible waste management, increasing community engagement, and improving local waste infrastructure.

Our goal is to create a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable environment for all residents.
